About Me

Mohammed Fahadh Khambrath, known professionally as MD. Fahadh MizhVer, is an Indian English Author recognized for his work in the psychological thriller genre. Born on May 31, 2008, in Malappuram, Kerala, India, he developed an early interest in the darker aspects of storytelling after a brief but formative period pursuing a career in music. When his artistic pursuits in the music industry were unexpectedly halted, he discovered a passion for crime thrillers and channeled his creative energies into writing.

At the age of seventeen, MizhVer published his debut book, a psychological thriller titled The Girl In The Walls. His shift from music to writing marked a critical point in his career, establishing him as a new author in the dark fiction genre.

The Khambrath Group was founded by Md. Fahadh MizhVer to handle his publishing work and future projects. The company remains a private publishing group, responsible for managing and producing his written works. It was created to ensure independent ownership and full creative control over his publications.
