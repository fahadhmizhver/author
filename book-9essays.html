9 Essays That Will Change The Way You Think

Author: MD. Fahadh MizhVer

Price: €8.99

9 Essays That Will Change The Way You Think by MD. Fahadh MizhVer is a sharp, provocative work that challenges the very structure of modern thought. It is not a self-help manual filled with comforting clichés. It is an intellectual reckoning—an invitation to confront the faulty logic that drives human behavior and to rebuild a stronger, more deliberate way of thinking.

Each essay dismantles a core illusion that governs everyday life: the belief that happiness waits at the end of achievement, that our convictions define truth, that comfort equals safety, or that identity is something to be discovered rather than constructed. Drawing from psychology, neuroscience, and clear reasoning, MizhVer examines how these illusions keep us chasing the next goal, defending the same opinions, and repeating the same mental errors.

This book does not promise peace or motivation. It offers clarity. It asks you to question what you believe, how you decide, and why you pursue what you do. The reward is not comfort—it is awareness. MizhVer's writing cuts through confusion with precision, leaving the reader with a disciplined understanding of how to think, not what to think.

9 Essays That Will Change The Way You Think is for readers who are ready to face the truth about their own mind and take control of it. It is for those who would rather confront hard ideas than live under easy illusions.


Publisher:

Khambrath Group is a private publishing company founded by MD. Fahadh MizhVer (Mohammed Fahadh Khambrath). Established to operate independently from mainstream distributors, the company focuses on developing, managing, and publishing literary works that explore human behavior, psychology, and modern authority.

The group's first major release, 9 Essays That Will Change The Way You Think, marked the beginning of its presence in the publishing field, gaining recognition for its structural and analytical approach to understanding human influence and control.

Khambrath Group functions as both a publishing entity and a creative management platform for Fahadh MizhVer's intellectual works. The company emphasizes ownership, originality, and the direct connection between author and audience—reflecting its founder's belief in independent creation and controlled distribution.
